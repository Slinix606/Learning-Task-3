🔊 MY TOP 10 ARTIST IN SPOTIFY 🎶

TOP 1

Kendrick Lamar

Kendrick Lamar is a critically acclaimed American rapper and songwriter celebrated for his deeply introspective, politically conscious lyrics and his profound storytelling about African-American culture

TOP 2

Hev Abi

Hev Abi is a highly successful and record-breaking Filipino hip-hop artist from Quezon City who rose to mainstream prominence with viral, genre-blending streaming hits like "Alam Mo Ba Girl".

TOP 3

Drake

Aubrey Drake Graham is a prolific and highly successful Canadian rapper, singer, and actor who transformed the modern music landscape by seamlessly blending introspective, melodic R&B sensibilities with sharp, narrative-driven hip-hop.

TOP 4

Don Toliver

Don Toliver is a versatile American rapper and singer-songwriter from Houston, Texas, who rose to prominence with his distinct, genre-blending sound that fuses psychedelic hip-hop, trap, and R&B.

TOP 5

Project Pat

VProject Pat (Patrick Earl Houston) is a highly influential Memphis-born rapper and cultural icon, best known for his gritty street tales, distinctive, heavily-sampled flows, and pivotal role as an early affiliate of the pioneering group Three 6 Mafia.

TOP 6

Bruno Mars

Bruno Mars is a multi-talented American singer-songwriter and record producer renowned for his retro showmanship, electrifying stage performances, and the ability to effortlessly blend pop, R&B, funk, and soul music styles.

TOP 7

Tame Impala

Tame Impala is the psychedelic music project of Australian multi-instrumentalist and producer Kevin Parker, who writes, records, and produces all of the music himself.

TOP 8

Michael Jackson

Michael Jackson was a pioneering American singer, songwriter, dancer, and philanthropist who revolutionized the global entertainment industry, broke profound racial barriers, and earned the title of the King of Pop through his unparalleled showmanship and monumental musical achievements.

TOP 9

Doja Cat

Doja Cat is a Grammy-winning American rapper, singer, and songwriter renowned for her genre-bending music, dynamic live performances, and humorous online presence.

TOP 10

Justin Bieber

Justin Bieber is a multi-platinum Canadian singer and songwriter who transitioned from a viral YouTube teen idol into one of the most successful and globally recognized pop artists of his generation.
